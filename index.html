  • Polearm Two-handed melee weapon with long handle.
    Including: Bardiche, Voulge, Scythe, Poleaxe, Halberd and War Scythe Not including: Spear, Trident, Brandistock, Spetum and Pike and its exceptional and elite versions
  • Staff Any not class specified staff, one- or two-handed.
    Not including: Eagle Orb, Sacred Globe, Smoked Sphere, Clasped Orb and even Jared's Stone and its exceptional and elite versions
  • Wand Necromancer specified weapon

  • Crossbow Missile weapon launching bolts
